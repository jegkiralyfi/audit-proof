Title: Local evaluation sample

Abstract
We conducted a randomized controlled study with a sample size of 120 participants (n = 120).

Methods
Participants were randomly assigned to intervention and control groups.
Raw data are available at https://example.org/data and the materials are available on request.

Results
We observed Cohen's d = 0.42, p = 0.03, 95% CI [0.10, 0.74].

Discussion
The intervention improved the outcome in this sample.
