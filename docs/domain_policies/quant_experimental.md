# Quantitative Experimental Reporting policy

This certification type is intentionally narrower than an entire discipline. It targets controlled or comparative quantitative studies in the social sciences and binds its first-tier audit to declared standards:

- APA JARS-Quant (2018), selected modules for general quantitative reporting, experimental designs, and random assignment
- TOP Guidelines (2015), selected modules for data/materials/preregistration signals

The lane should be treated as a certification type, not as a claim to audit all of psychology or all social science.
